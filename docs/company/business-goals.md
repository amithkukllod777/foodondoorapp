## North-star

Nutriwow ko profitable, repeat-driven D2C dry-fruits brand banana — apni independent infra par (TiDB + Vercel), bina platform lock-in ke.

## Objectives

1. **Revenue growth** — top-line GMV badhana (D2C + naye channels).
2. **Profitability** — healthy contribution margin per order; discounting control.
3. **Retention** — repeat purchase rate aur AOV improve karna (combos, subscriptions).
4. **Expansion** — naye sales channels (marketplaces / quick-commerce) — detail [`../marketing/campaigns.md`](../marketing/campaigns.md).
