Planning grid for content across blog, WhatsApp, social and email. Existing channels in repo:

(see `WHATSAPP_CAMPAIGNS_HANDOFF.md`), GA4 `G-N1EESY3X9F` + Meta Pixel/CAPI for attribution.

## Strategic intent

- **Serves priorities:** brand awareness (top-funnel reach) → owned-audience growth (WhatsApp/email opt-ins) → conversion (tie content to PDPs + coupons).
- **Content mix (enforced):** **70% Educational · 20% Trust · 10% Promotional.** Never pure product spam.
- **Golden rule:** every post must **Educate, Entertain, Build Trust, or Drive Action** — and link to a real asset (blog/PDP/collection) for SEO + remarketing signal.

| Channel | Cadence | Primary format | Role |

| Instagram | 1 hero/day + 1–2 Stories/day | Reels (60%), carousels (30%), static (10%) | Reach + saves + community |
