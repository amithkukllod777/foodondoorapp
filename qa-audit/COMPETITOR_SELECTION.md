## Selected competitors

Selection is based on category, target user, geography, business model and pricing segment — **not** popularity alone. All three are Indian D2C dry-fruit/nut brands running their own online stores in the same consumer segment.

| # | Competitor | Type | Platform | Why relevant |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Happilo** (happilo.com) | Market leader / direct | Shopify | Largest premium D2C dry-fruit brand in India; same category & premium positioning; benchmark for table-stakes storefront features (accounts, subscribe & save, reviews, gifting hub). |
| 2 | **Farmley** (farmley.com) | Emerging challenger / direct | Shopify | Fast-growing D2C dry-fruit + innovative-snacking brand; direct overlap on core SKUs and gifting; strong on modern snacking formats. |
| 3 | **Nutraj** (nutraj.com) | Established direct competitor | Shopify | Long-established (90+ yr heritage) dry-fruit brand with own D2C store; broad SKU range and gifting hampers; represents the "trust/heritage" competitor. |

## Selection rationale

- **Directly comparable** on category, region (India), business model (own D2C store), and pricing segment (mid-premium). Not compared against marketplaces (Amazon/Flipkart/BigBasket) or quick-commerce (Blinkit/Zepto), which are distribution channels rather than directly-comparable D2C storefronts.
- All three run on **Shopify**, which matters: it gives them a large set of table-stakes storefront capabilities "for free" via the Shopify platform + app ecosystem (customer accounts, address book, order history, guest→account, Subscribe & Save apps, review apps like Judge.me, loyalty apps, gift cards). This is the honest baseline Nutriwow (a custom build) is measured against.
